CAMPUS AMBASSADOR

Lead the Tech Revolution at your Campus

[ OVERVIEW ]

Be the Face of AAYAM

The AAYAM Campus Ambassador (CA) program is a unique opportunity for student leaders to represent our techfest in their respective colleges. As a CA, you will be the primary point of contact between AAYAM and your campus.

[ RESPONSIBILITIES ]

Your Mission

  • Promote AAYAM events across your college networks.
  • Facilitate student registrations for various competitions.
  • Serve as the official representative of NST Techfest.
[ PERKS & REWARDS ]
  • Exclusive AAYAM merchandise and swags.
  • Official AAYAM gear, swag, and exclusive artifacts.
  • Unrestricted access to all AAYAM sectors and events.
  • Instant communication with organizers and exclusive updates.
  • Top performers get bonus credits, hardware, and special status.
  • Connect with sponsors, mentors, and industry leaders.

FAQ

Q:

Who can become an ambassador?

Any college/university student passionate about technology and events can apply. No prior experience needed!

Q:

How do I track my progress?

You'll get a unique link and access to a dashboard showing your real-time recruitment and tier progress.

Q:

When do I receive my rewards?

Gear is dispatched as you hit each tier. Final rewards are distributed at the event or within 2 weeks after.

Q:

Can I be an ambassador from any college?

Absolutely! Our program is open to students from across India. Promote AAYAM anywhere!
