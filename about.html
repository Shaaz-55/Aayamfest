ABOUT AAYAM

The Vision Behind The Fest

[ ORIGINS ]

History & Organizing College

Organized by the brilliant minds at Newton School of Technology (NST), AAYAM was born from a desire to bridge the gap between academic learning and bleeding-edge industry innovation. It rapidly transformed from a collegiate exhibition into Asia's premier tech congregation.

[ VISION & MISSION ]

Empowering Builders

Our mission is to cultivate an ecosystem where bold ideas meet execution. We believe that constraints breed creativity, and through our rigorous hackathons and challenges, we aim to uncover the next generation of tech pioneers.

[ HIGHLIGHT ]

Past Achievements

  • Hosted India's largest localized Web3 hackathon in 2025.
  • Over 5,000+ total alumni participants now working at top-tier FAANG companies and rising startups.
  • ₹5,00,000+ awarded in total prize money over the last 3 editions.
  • Official partnerships built with Google Space, AWS, and Polygon.
[ THIS YEAR'S THEME ]

Enter Another Dimension

As AI reshapes reality and quantum computing breaks barriers, we are crossing the event horizon. AAYAM '26 explores the profound shifts happening in our technological fabric.
