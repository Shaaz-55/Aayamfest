[ OVERVIEW ]

Be the Face of AAYAM

The AAYAM Campus Ambassador (CA) program is a unique opportunity for student leaders to represent our techfest in their respective colleges. As a CA, you will be the primary point of contact between AAYAM and your campus.

[ RESPONSIBILITIES ]

Your Mission

  • Promote AAYAM events across your college networks.
  • Facilitate student registrations for various competitions.
  • Serve as the official representative of NST Techfest.
[ PERKS & REWARDS ]
  • Exclusive AAYAM merchandise and swags.
  • Certificate of Distinction for top-performing CAs.
  • Free VIP access to prime workshops and events.
  • Direct networking opportunities with industry sponsors.
